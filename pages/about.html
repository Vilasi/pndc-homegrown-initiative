Thanks for Visiting!

Building a good life should be accessible to everybody

We're The Future Leaders organization - a part of the Mercer County Homegrown Initiative by Penn-Northwest Co. The Future Leaders are a group of young business leaders within the Mercer County area who are dedicated to improving the quality of life in our region.

Our mission is to provide the necessary resources for young adults to thrive in our community. Using our connections with local businesses, and the renowned colleges and trade schools in our area, we work to facilitate a portal through which everyone can connect with the opportunities that exist right here around us.
