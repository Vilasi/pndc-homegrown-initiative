Play Easy

Whether you enjoy quiet, serene outdoor adventures or an upbeat night life, Mercer County has it all. Take a stroll through Buhl Park, explore nearly 20 state game lands, enjoy the eclectic restaurant scene, or spend time in the bustling downtown of Sharon or Grove City. Residents also take advantage of the convenient 1-hour drive to Cleveland, Erie, and Pittsburgh.
